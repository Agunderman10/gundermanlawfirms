Complaints

If you, as the client, have any complaints about Gunderman Law Firms that cannot be resolved you may get in contact with your local or state bar association. As our client, you have the right to sue any lawyer for serious errors that resulted in your injury or loss. This type of case is known as a legal malpractice case. To handle these types of situations, some state bar associations have arbitration systems in which hearings are held to weigh the disagreement and issue opinions. Some disputes deal with the amount of the fee charged for the lawyer's services. Panels may order the attorney to return some of a client's money if they decide the fee was improper. At Gunderman Law Firms you can have the confidence that your complaints will be heard and dealt with smoothly.
